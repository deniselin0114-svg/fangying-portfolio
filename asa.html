Authoring and monitoring real-time analytics jobs in the Azure portal — query, test, and ship.

Problem to solve

Authoring streaming jobs felt risky

Writing and shipping a Stream Analytics job meant editing queries with little feedback until the job ran against live data. Users wanted to test confidently before committing to production.

  • Hard to validate query logic before running against live input.
  • Monitoring and authoring lived in separate mental models.
  • Errors were costly to discover after deployment.
Research & validation

What we learned from users

Sessions with analytics engineers showed that a tight author → test → monitor loop in one place built confidence and reduced production incidents caused by untested query changes.
