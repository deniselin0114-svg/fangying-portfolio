Schematize-E2E-flow

An end-to-end schema-aware flow — from source schema to typed destination, validated every step of the way.

✸
Problem to solve

Schema drift broke pipelines silently

Without an end-to-end view of schema, a change at the source could quietly break everything downstream. Users needed the schema to be a first-class, visible part of the flow — not an afterthought.

  • Schema changes propagated invisibly and broke destinations.
  • No single source of truth for the shape of the data.
  • Debugging meant tracing failures backwards through the pipeline.
Research & validation

What we learned from users

Concept testing showed that making schema explicit and validating it at each hop gave users confidence to evolve their pipelines without fear of silent breakage.
